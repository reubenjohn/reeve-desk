# Goals: Your North Star

This file defines your long-term objectives - the "why" behind your daily activities. These are the outcomes that matter most to you.

**How to use this file:**
- Keep it short (3-5 active goals maximum)
- Make goals specific and measurable when possible
- Review and update quarterly or when priorities shift
- Reeve will reference these daily to help you stay aligned

**Format:**
Each goal should have:
- **Title** - Clear, concise name
- **Why** - The deeper reason this matters to you
- **Success Criteria** - How you'll know you achieved it
- **Status** - Active, On Hold, Completed, Cancelled
- **Target Date** - When you want to achieve this (if applicable)
