## Design Philosophy

**Why Markdown files?**
- Human-readable and editable (no databases, no GUIs required)
- Version-controllable with git (optional)
- Platform-independent
- Future-proof (plain text never becomes obsolete)

**The "Glass Box" Principle:**
- Everything Reeve knows is visible here
- No hidden algorithms or black-box behavior
- User can audit and modify anything
- Trust through transparency

## Template Evolution

**Short term (1-2 weeks):**

**Medium term (1-3 months):**

**Long term (3+ months):**
